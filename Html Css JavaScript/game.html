Game Rules

Goal: Reach the winning score before your opponent.

Start: Both players begin with zero score.

Scoring: Each click adds one point to your score.

Winning: First to reach the winning score wins.

Winning Score: Set before the game starts.

Reset: After finishing the game, start again by pressing enter.

Shift Key: Press to increase your score by one.

History: Tracks winners of last five games.

Shift Key Use: Left Shift for Player 1, Right Shift for Player 2.

History Display: Shows last five winners.
